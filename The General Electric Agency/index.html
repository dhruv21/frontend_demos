The General Electric Agency

About US

India's largest dealer in rotating machine & drives

Establishment in 1939, Agency was originally named The General Electric store in Khambhat, Gujarat by pioneer Late Shri Mafatlal Ambalal Patel. In 1989, the name evolved to The General Electric Agency, expanding its operations to Ankleshwar, Gujarat by Late Shri Navinchandra Mafatlal Patel. The subsequent management of the agency was entrusted to his sons, Shri Piyush Navinchandra Patel and Mr. Nimesh Navinchandra Patel.
